MY MEDDIES - Deployment Instructions

To deploy to Netlify (FREE):

1. Go to: https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag and drop THIS ENTIRE FOLDER into the drop zone
3. Your site will be live in seconds!
4. You'll get a URL like: random-name-123456.netlify.app
5. You can customize the URL in Netlify settings

That's it! Your My Meddies app will be accessible from anywhere.

Files included:
- nikki-meds-dashboard.html (Medication Tracker)
- nikki-meds-safety.html (Drug Safety Checker)
- nikki-meds.json (Your medication log)
- lqts-drug-database.json (52 medications database)
- med-icon.jpeg (Cute frog icon)
